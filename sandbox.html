What this demo shows

Every AgDR record captures the complete PPP triplet — Provenance, Place, and Purpose — at the exact inference instant. The supervariable adapts the triplet to any domain while maintaining the same atomic structure. In production, each record is cryptographically signed with BLAKE3 + Ed25519 and chained in a forward-secret Merkle tree.

This is a client-side simulation. Production AgDR records are generated server-side with hardware-backed signing. Get Started with the SDK to generate real records.
