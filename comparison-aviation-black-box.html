Beyond the Black Box.

The aviation black box solved the 20th century's accountability crisis. AgDR v1.8 solves the autonomous intelligence crisis of the 21st—not by recording the crash, but by recording the reasoning.

Functional Analysis: FDR/CVR vs. AgDR

While the aviation industry relies on physical recovery and post-incident investigation, AgDR utilizes Atomic Kernel Inference to ensure every decision is tamper-proof from the moment of birth.

Dimension Aviation (FDR + CVR) AgDR v1.8 (AKI) The Edge

Trigger Continuous loop (last 2–25 hours); data is overwritten. Immutable capture of every single inference, stored forever. TOTALITY

The Fundamental Shift

"The black box was built to find blame after a tragedy. AgDR was built to provide accountability for every success, preventing the tragedy from ever losing its context."

Why Mathematical Certainty Wins

In a courtroom, a physical black box can be damaged or lost. An AgDR record, protected by BLAKE3 hashing and kernel-level invariants, is mathematically impossible to repudiate. It moves the conversation from "What do we think happened?" to "Check the record."
