The AI Patient Chart.

Applying the rigorous non-repudiation and lifecycle standards of Electronic Health Records (EHR) to autonomous inference.

Comparative Standards

Metric Medical Record (PHIPA) AgDR v1.8 (AKI)
Audit Trail Who accessed/changed the record and when. Cryptographic proof of every inference and human delta.
Non-Repudiation Electronic or physical signature by provider. Hardware-bound BLAKE3 hash and Ed25519 signature.
Retention Typically 10 years or 10 years past majority. Immutable Merkle chain; indefinitely verifiable.
Integrity Access logs and version history. Forward-secret Merkle chaining; tamper-evident by design.
Legal Admissibility Admissible under health privacy statutes. Hardened for CEA s.31.1 as a system-integrity record.

AgDR brings clinical-grade forensic integrity to AI governance. Just as a patient chart must be complete, contemporaneous, and tamper-evident, every autonomous decision requires the same standard of care.
