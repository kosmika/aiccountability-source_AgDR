Built on Existing Law

No new legislation required. AgDR maps directly to frameworks courts and regulators already rely on.

Canada Evidence Act

Atomic capture satisfies the business records reliability test. Admissible without expert reconstruction.
CBCA s.122 — Fiduciary Duty

The FOI terminal node maps directly to directors' duty of care. Boards can prove AI oversight under existing corporate law.
CBSA Export Control

Provenance + Place + Purpose triplet satisfies CBSA traceability requirements. Audit time from weeks to minutes.
EU AI Act (2024/1689)

AgDR directly implements Article 12 (record-keeping) and Article 14 (human oversight) for high-risk AI systems.
PHIPA / PIPEDA

The Place pillar captures purpose-limitation at inference time — satisfying privacy law's most demanding requirement.
Aviation Black Box Standard

AgDR does for AI what the FDR/CVR did for aviation — except it doesn't wait for the crash.
