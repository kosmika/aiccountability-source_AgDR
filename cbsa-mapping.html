The Legal Benefit for Boards

By implementing the AgDR standard, a Board of Directors establishes a contemporaneous record admissible under Section 31.1 of the Canada Evidence Act. This allows the corporation to prove, mathematically, that it exercised due diligence even if the AI's output is later challenged.

Operational Fiduciary Duty

AgDR moves corporate governance from "Reactive Policy" to "Proactive Record-Keeping." Instead of reviewing logs months after a crisis, the Final Oversight Integrity (FOI) role provides real-time, terminal accountability for high-risk autonomous inferences.
