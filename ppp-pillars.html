Pillars.

The PPP Triplet is the minimum viable data required for a defensible AI inference.

01

Purpose

Every inference begins with an intent. Purpose defines the "Why" and the closing principles—Beauty, Truth, and Wisdom. This is not metadata; it is a boundary condition for the kernel.

Statutory Anchor

Satisfies the Business Judgment Rule by proving the decision was made on an informed, intentional basis.

02

Place

Place captures the hardware and regulatory jurisdiction of the compute node. It anchors the digital act to a physical territory.

Statutory Anchor

Enforces CEA s.31.2 (System Integrity). It proves the record-keeping system was functioning in a known, authorized environment.

03

Person

Person identifies the authorized agent or Fiduciary Officer. This is the cryptographic "hand" that signs the record into existence.

Statutory Anchor

Maps to Bills of Exchange Act s.4 (Signature). It ensures the inference is an unconditional, non-repudiable order.
