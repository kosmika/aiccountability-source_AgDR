CBCA Fiduciary Mapping

How AgDR v0.2 Maps to Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA) s.122

CBCA s.122 Duty How AgDR Satisfies It AgDR Component Used
Act honestly and in good faith Purpose is captured verbatim at inference instant Purpose pillar
Exercise the care, diligence and skill Binary human delta chain + FOI escalation proves meaningful human oversight Human Delta + FOI
Act in the best interests of the corporation Place defines the intended lawful outcome and success boundary Place pillar
Consider stakeholders Provenance records all affected stakeholders and WIIFM context Provenance pillar

Key Legal Benefit
The atomic capture + FOI terminal role creates an auditable chain that directly satisfies directors’ fiduciary obligations. Boards can now prove they met their CBCA duties even when agents act autonomously.

This mapping makes AgDR the natural evidentiary layer for corporate Canada.
