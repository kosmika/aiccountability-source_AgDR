Human-Machine Interface

The Human Delta Chain.

Not merely human-in-the-loop. The Human Delta Chain captures the variance between machine output and human judgment through the Wisdom lens of the PPP Triplet, creating an auditable record for compliance, training, and fiduciary control.

CBCA s.122 COMPLIANCE

Fiduciary Office Intervenor (FOI)

The FOI is a specific, designated role in the AgDR ecosystem. As the terminal node of the Human Delta Chain, the FOI exercises fiduciary duty under CBCA s.122 by reviewing the Human Delta Chain to verify that the PPP Triplet (Provenance, Place, Purpose) was not just present, but appropriate for the context. This satisfies the non-delegable fiduciary duty of care for high-risk AI systems under Canadian corporate law.

Clarification: FOI stands for Fiduciary Office Intervenor. This is a defined role, not a generic concept. The FOI is the human authority who intervenes at the terminal point of the decision record chain to affirm, modify, or halt autonomous actions in accordance with fiduciary obligations.

The Human Delta Chain: Beyond "Human-in-the-Loop"

Industry has preached "human-in-the-loop" for eons. The Human Delta Chain is different. It is not a checkpoint. It is a captured component of human intervention, cryptographically sealed at the moment of variance, intended for three distinct purposes:

  • Compliance Evidence: A court-admissible record proving that human oversight occurred, when it occurred, and why, satisfying statutory requirements like CBCA s.122 and EU AI Act Article 14.
  • Model Training Feedback: Structured variance data that enables supervised refinement of autonomous systems, turning oversight into improvement without compromising audit integrity.
  • Fiduciary Control Enforcement: A mechanism for the FOI to exercise duty of care in real time, with every intervention recorded, signed, and chained to the original decision record.

Wisdom as the Evaluative Lens

Within the PPP Triplet, Purpose is operationalized through the Beauty-Truth-Wisdom framework. Wisdom is the component that evaluates whether a machine's output, however technically correct (Truth) or elegantly designed (Beauty), aligns with human-defined purpose in context. The Human Delta Chain captures the moment Wisdom intervenes.

Delta State Wisdom Evaluation FOI Action CBCA s.122 Alignment
Delta Zero (Pass) Wisdom affirms: machine output aligns with Purpose in context. FOI cryptographically signs affirmation; record sealed with zero variance. Active fiduciary consent: duty of care satisfied through documented affirmation of alignment.
Positive Delta (Mod) Wisdom detects misalignment: output is technically sound but contextually inappropriate. FOI steers the system via a documented, cryptographically sealed intervention; delta recorded as structured feedback. Corrective duty of care: fiduciary obligation to adjust course when machine output deviates from corporate best interests.
Interrupt (Halt) Wisdom identifies risk: continued autonomy threatens purpose or public interest. FOI triggers the AKI Gate to halt escalation; intervention recorded as emergency mitigation with full context. Emergency mitigation: fiduciary duty to prevent harm when autonomous systems threaten corporate or public interest.

Three-Purpose Architecture

The Human Delta Chain is designed to serve three masters without compromise:

  1. Compliance: Every delta is a forensic record. The chain provides immutable proof that human oversight occurred, satisfying evidentiary standards under CEA s.31.1 and enabling efficient regulatory audit.
  2. Training: Structured delta data (not raw logs) feeds model refinement pipelines. Because each intervention is Wisdom-evaluated and FOI-signed, training data carries provenance and purpose context, reducing drift and improving alignment.
  3. Fiduciary Control: The FOI exercises duty of care through the chain. Every affirmation, modification, or halt is a fiduciary act, recorded with timestamp, context, and cryptographic proof, satisfying the non-delegable nature of CBCA s.122.
The Human Delta Chain does not ask whether a human was present. It records what the human judged, why they judged it, and how that judgment aligns with Wisdom-defined Purpose. That is the difference between theater and accountability.
